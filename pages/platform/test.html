    • Buying a miner using the exchange

      Buying artonline from exchange is simular to buying it from the mining app.
      Go to the market and select a preferable miner. Press "buy" button right under preferable miner and confirm the transaction. You should recieve the purchased miner within 5 minutes.

      Listing from wallet


      Go tomining platformand go to wallet. Select a pool of mining Card you want to list on exchange. Select the mining card you want to list. Press on the trianglenext to your mining card id. Enter
      0x535e67270f4feb15bffbfe86fee308b81799a7a5
      in "currency to recieve" field if you would like to recieve artonline. Enter 0x0 if you would like to recieve BNB. Enter the price of your miner (it can be any price) in "price" field. Press "list" button and approve the transaction. Your miner will get listed on exchange within 10 minutes.

      Getting a mining card

      Using mining platform requires you to have a mining card wich are available on the exchange. There is also an exchange on the mining platform. Celect a preferable mining card and press buy.
      A pop up metamask window should appear. Press confirm, the card you bought is now yours. If it didnt happen, make sure the transaction was successful.

      Activating the card

      Go to Pools and select the pool of your mining card. Your card should load within 30 seconds.
      Press the pink triangle next to your miner. Confirm the transaction and voila! Your card is now activated! To deactivate the card do the same thing.
      If a user holds an ArtOnline NFT Graphics Card, and that card is activated, it will continue to provide passive returns until the card is de-activated. After de-activation the user has the option to sell at premium prices, sell for less on the exchange or, (coming soon) auction the NFT to the highest bidder.

      Spinner

      A spinner can be activated on each nft gpu, It increases mining by 1% and costs 3500 art to activate. In a pool of 3 GPUs you can activate up to 3 spinners for a total of 3% mining bonus on the pool. So if you have 5 modules you can only activate 5 spinners. If the spinner is acrivated there is a fan image next to the miner.

      Claiming rewards

      To claim the rewards go to the mining pool and press "Get reward" button.
      These 2 buttons will pop up. Claim now (20% tax) means 20% of your mined artonline will get deducted (15% burned and 5% credited to the staking pool), but you get the reward right after transaction. So if you mined 10000 artonline you only get 8000.
      Stake for 6 months means you wont be able to use your artonline for 6 months, but no tax.
      Staking rewards are based on when people take the 20% tax option. The more people that take mining fees instantly, the more you will get from staking. Everyone gets the same split too, so even small stakers earn the same amount as whales. A user who only has one stake gets the same as a user with two stakes. Or even one for 10k gets the same as 1k.

      Using calculator

      Calculator shows how much artonline will be mined in selected amount of time. Select your pool in "GPU" field. Enter current pool size for selected miner in "miners" field. Enter amount of your miners in "GPU'S" field. You get the result in Art/min.
